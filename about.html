FOUNDATION OF LA GRAZIA

La Grazia is an Italian contemporary perfumer & businessman who was made famous at the age of 26 designing the legendary Le Male for Jean Paul Gaultier. This initial success has been followed by over 40 creations for major perfume brands across the world including Elie Saab Le Parfum, My Burberry & Narciso Rodriguez for Her. Grazia had decided at the young age of 15 that he wanted to be a perfumer, despite a complete lack of knowledge about the profession. However, he quickly understood that it would be replete with discipline and perseverance – values he learned during years of piano and ballet lessons.

FRAGRANCES OF LA GRAZIA

La Grazia is a high-end fragrance brand, founded in 2019 by the renowned perfumer Mrs. Grazia. Grazia is considered a master perfumer, having created over 40 fragrances for some of the world's most prestigious brands. The brand La Grazia offers a range of luxury perfumes and home fragrances that are both elegant and sophisticated. The brand's products are known for their attention to detail, as well as their use of high-quality ingredients and innovative fragrances.

FACTS ABOUT LA GRAZIA

One of the interesting things about La Grazia is its approach to fragrance creation. The brand takes a unique and creative approach to fragrance creation, combining traditional techniques with contemporary styles to create fragrances that are both timeless and modern. Each fragrance is carefully crafted, with Kurkdjian personally involved in every aspect of the process from concept to final product. This ensures that each fragrance is of the highest quality, and that it reflects Grazia's passion for fragrance.
